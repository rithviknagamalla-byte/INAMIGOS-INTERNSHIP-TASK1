Our Purpose

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating sustainable social impact across India through education, empowerment, community service, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and skill development.

  • ✔ Section 8 Registered NGO
  • ✔ 80G & 12A Certified
  • ✔ CSR-1 Registered
  • ✔ NITI Aayog Registered
  • ✔ ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • ✔ Operating Across India
🤝

Our Promise

We believe in the power of collective action and remain committed to transparency, accountability, and measurable impact through meaningful initiatives that uplift communities and create lasting change.

  • ✔ 50,000+ Meals Distributed through Project SEVA
  • ✔ 20,000+ Saplings Planted through Project PRAKRITI
  • ✔ 50+ Stray Animals Fed Daily through Project JEEV
  • ✔ Women Empowerment through Project UDAAN
  • ✔ Educational Support through Project BACHPANSHALA
  • ✔ 30,000+ Interns Trained through Project VIKAS
